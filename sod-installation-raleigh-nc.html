Best Time to Install Sod in Raleigh

In Raleigh's climate, warm-season sod (Bermuda, Zoysia) is best installed from April through August when soil temperatures are warm and growth is active. Cool-season Tall Fescue sod is best installed in fall (September–November) or spring. We source fresh sod and can typically schedule installation within 1–2 weeks of your estimate.

Grass Varieties

Sod Varieties We Install in Raleigh, NC

We'll recommend the best grass for your specific yard conditions, sun exposure, and lifestyle.

☀️
Bermudagrass
Warm Season — Most Popular

The gold standard for sunny Raleigh yards. Bermuda is extremely durable, drought-tolerant once established, and grows thick and dense. Ideal for high-traffic areas, kids, and pets. Goes dormant (brown) in winter, green in summer.

🌿
Zoysiagrass
Warm Season — Premium Choice

Zoysia is a premium warm-season grass with a finer texture and better shade tolerance than Bermuda. It forms a dense, carpet-like lawn that chokes out weeds naturally and holds up well in Raleigh's climate. Slightly slower to establish but worth the wait.

🍃
Tall Fescue
Cool Season — Best for Shade

Tall Fescue is the most widely used lawn grass in Raleigh and stays green through winter — a big advantage in NC. It tolerates shade better than warm-season grasses, making it ideal for lots with trees. Best installed in fall for best results.

Sod installation in Raleigh typically costs $1.00–$2.00 per square foot for materials and $0.50–$1.00 per sq ft for labor, depending on the grass type and site prep needed. A typical 2,000 sq ft yard runs $3,000–$6,000 installed. We provide free estimates with exact pricing for your property.

In Raleigh's climate, sod typically takes 2–3 weeks to root firmly during the active growing season (spring/summer for warm-season grasses, fall for fescue). You'll see the sod knit to the soil when you can no longer easily lift a corner. We provide a detailed watering schedule to maximize establishment success.

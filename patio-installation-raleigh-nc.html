Patio Installation in Raleigh, NC — Expand Your Living Space Outdoors

Raleigh's mild climate is perfect for outdoor living — and a professionally installed patio gives you the space to enjoy it. Whether you envision a simple seating area off the back door or a full outdoor entertainment zone with a kitchen, fire pit, and dining space, GreenVista designs and installs patios that become the most-used part of your home.

We take patio installation in Raleigh, NC seriously. That means proper base preparation — excavating to the right depth, compacting gravel, setting sand bedding — before the first paver is placed. A well-built patio in Raleigh can last 30+ years without sinking, shifting, or cracking. A poorly built one starts showing problems in 2–3 years.

Paver Patios vs. Concrete vs. Flagstone

We primarily install paver patios because pavers outperform poured concrete in Raleigh's climate — they flex slightly with ground movement, individual pavers can be replaced if damaged, and they offer vastly more design options. Flagstone patios offer a more natural, irregular look that works beautifully in garden-style settings. We'll help you choose the right material for your home, budget, and aesthetic.

What's Included in Our Patio Installation

  • Free design consultation and patio layout
  • Excavation to proper depth (typically 8–10 inches)
  • Compacted gravel base (at least 6 inches)
  • Bedding sand layer and screed
  • Professional paver installation and jointing
  • Polymeric sand application and compaction
  • Edge restraint installation to prevent spreading
  • Final cleanup and sealing (optional)
Patio Styles

Patio Types We Install in Raleigh, NC

Choose from a wide range of materials and styles — we'll help you find the perfect fit for your home and budget.

🪨
Concrete Paver Patios

The most popular choice in Raleigh — concrete pavers come in dozens of colors, shapes, and textures. Extremely durable, freeze-thaw resistant, and available in patterns from simple running bond to complex herringbone. Price range: $15–$22/sq ft installed.

🪨
Natural Flagstone Patios

Bluestone, slate, or limestone flagstone creates a sophisticated, natural look. Each stone is unique, creating an organic, garden-feel patio that ages beautifully. Perfect for formal gardens and cottage-style homes. Price range: $20–$35/sq ft installed.

🧱
Brick Paver Patios

Tumbled clay brick pavers offer a warm, classic look that complements traditional Raleigh homes beautifully. They age gracefully and are extremely durable. A timeless choice that never goes out of style.

🏡
Multi-Level Patios

For sloped or large yards, multi-level patios connected by steps or retaining walls create distinct outdoor "rooms" — a dining level, a lounge level, maybe a fire pit level. We design and build these complex projects with ease.

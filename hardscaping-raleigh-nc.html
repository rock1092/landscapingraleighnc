Hardscaping Raleigh NC — Common Questions

Paver patio installation in Raleigh typically costs $15–$30 per square foot installed, depending on paver type, pattern complexity, and base preparation needs. A 300 sq ft patio runs roughly $4,500–$9,000. We provide free, detailed estimates so you know exactly what you're getting.

A properly installed paver patio in Raleigh can last 25–50+ years. The key is correct base depth (at least 6" of compacted gravel) and proper edge restraints. We don't skip steps — every patio we build is engineered to handle NC's wet winters and dry summers without shifting.

Most residential patio and walkway projects don't require permits in Raleigh. Retaining walls over 30" in exposed height typically require a permit and engineered design. We handle all permit research and applications as part of our project process.

For Raleigh's climate with hot summers and occasional freezing winters, we recommend concrete pavers (very durable, freeze-thaw resistant), natural bluestone or flagstone, or manufactured stone. We avoid poured concrete surfaces for patios as they crack more easily than pavers in our climate.

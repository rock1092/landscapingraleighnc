What's Included in Our Lawn Care Service

Our standard lawn mowing Raleigh NC service includes much more than just cutting the grass. Every visit we:

  • Mow at the correct height for your grass type (Bermuda, Fescue, Zoysia, etc.)
  • Edge all sidewalks, driveways, and bed borders cleanly
  • Trim around fences, trees, and obstacles with a string trimmer
  • Blow all clippings off hard surfaces — driveways, patios, and walkways
  • Inspect for problem areas: bare spots, pest damage, disease, or drainage issues

Year-Round Lawn Maintenance Programs

A great lawn isn't built in a single season — it's maintained with a smart, consistent program. Our yard maintenance Raleigh packages include fertilization, weed control, aeration, overseeding, and seasonal cleanup so your lawn looks great every month of the year.

In spring, we focus on weed pre-emergent applications and early fertilization. Summer brings regular mowing and spot weed control. Fall is critical for aeration and overseeding cool-season grasses. In winter, we perform cleanup, scalping, and pre-emergent prep so you're ahead come spring.

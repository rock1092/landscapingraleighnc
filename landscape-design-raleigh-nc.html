Landscape Design Raleigh NC — Frequently Asked Questions

Landscape design costs vary based on property size and project complexity. Design fees for residential projects in Raleigh typically range from $300–$1,500 for the design plan. Installation costs depend on plant selections, quantity, and scope. We offer free initial consultations and provide detailed quotes before any work begins.

Raleigh is in USDA Plant Hardiness Zone 7b. We recommend native and adapted plants that thrive here: Crepe Myrtle, Oakleaf Hydrangea, Knockout Roses, Liriope, Nandina (heavenly bamboo), Loropetalum, River Birch trees, native ornamental grasses, and Encore Azaleas. These plants handle our hot summers, clay soils, and occasional winter freezes beautifully.

From initial consultation to finished installation, most residential landscape design projects in Raleigh take 3–8 weeks. The design phase takes 1–2 weeks, plant sourcing 1–2 weeks, and installation typically 1–3 days depending on project scope. Larger or phased projects may take longer.

We handle everything from design through installation — you work with one company the whole way. Our installation crews are experienced, use proper planting techniques, and leave your yard clean and beautiful. We also offer a plant warranty on all installed materials.

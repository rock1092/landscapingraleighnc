Irrigation Installation in Raleigh, NC — Smart Watering for Healthier Lawns

Watering your Raleigh lawn and garden by hand or with portable sprinklers is inefficient, uneven, and time-consuming. A properly designed and installed irrigation system in Raleigh, NC waters the right zones at the right times for the right duration — automatically — so you get a healthier lawn while actually using less water.

GreenVista designs irrigation systems specific to your property's layout, sun exposure, soil type, and plant material. We zone systems to match watering needs — lawn areas need different coverage and run times than shrub beds, and shaded areas need less water than sunny spots. A well-designed system eliminates dry spots, prevents overwatering, and takes the guesswork out of irrigation entirely.

Sprinkler Systems for Raleigh Lawns

Our in-ground sprinkler systems in Raleigh, NC use rotary and spray heads sized to each zone for complete, even coverage. We use professional-grade components from Rain Bird and Hunter — not big-box store parts — that last years longer and perform reliably through Raleigh's hot summers.

Drip Irrigation for Beds & Gardens

Drip irrigation delivers water directly to root zones in plant beds with zero waste from overspray or evaporation. Ideal for mulched beds, vegetable gardens, and areas where precision matters. We install drip systems as standalone installations or as zones within a larger irrigation system.

Smart Controller Technology

We install Wi-Fi-enabled smart controllers that adjust watering schedules based on local weather data — automatically skipping irrigation when rain is coming and adjusting run times based on temperature and humidity. Smart controllers typically reduce water usage by 30–50% versus basic timer controllers.

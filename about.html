Our Story

From a Pickup Truck
to Raleigh's #1 Landscapers

GreenVista Landscaping started in 2012 with one crew, one truck, and a commitment to doing better work than the competition. Founder Marcus Delaney had spent years working for large national landscaping franchises and watched firsthand how absentee ownership led to sloppy work, no-shows, and customers who felt like account numbers — not neighbors.

So Marcus went independent. He launched GreenVista right here in Raleigh, NC and took on a handful of residential lawn care accounts in the Brier Creek and North Hills neighborhoods. Word spread fast. Within two years, he had a second crew. By 2017, GreenVista had grown to serve commercial properties, HOAs, and hundreds of homeowners across Wake County.

Today, GreenVista employs a full team of licensed, trained landscaping professionals — and Marcus still personally reviews every new customer estimate. We're not a franchise. We're your neighbors. And we treat your yard the way we'd want ours treated.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

Everything we do at GreenVista comes back to these four principles. They guide how we hire, how we work, and how we treat our customers.

🎯

Quality Over Volume

We'd rather do 10 jobs really well than 100 jobs carelessly. Every property gets the same attention whether it's a small residential lawn or a large commercial account.

🤝

Honest Communication

If there's a problem, we tell you. If a job will take longer than expected, we call ahead. No surprises on invoices. No excuses when something goes wrong — just solutions.

🌿

Environmental Responsibility

We select plants native to the Piedmont region whenever possible, recommend water-efficient irrigation, and compost or recycle yard waste. Good landscaping and good stewardship go hand in hand.

📅

Reliability You Can Count On

If your lawn is scheduled for Tuesday, we're there Tuesday. Raleigh weather doesn't always cooperate, but we communicate immediately and reschedule the same week whenever possible.

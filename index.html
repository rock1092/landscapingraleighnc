Best Grass Types for Raleigh, NC Lawns

Raleigh's climate sits in the "transition zone" — making grass selection critical. Tall Fescue is the most popular choice in Wake County, staying green year-round. Bermuda grass thrives in full-sun Raleigh yards, while Zoysia offers great drought tolerance. Our Raleigh landscaping experts will recommend the perfect grass type for your yard's sun, soil, and water conditions.

When to Fertilize Your Lawn in Raleigh, NC

For Tall Fescue lawns in Raleigh, fertilize in September and November for best results. Warm-season grasses like Bermuda and Zoysia in Wake County should be fertilized May through July. Never fertilize during a drought — Raleigh summers can be brutal. Our Raleigh lawn care team handles all fertilization and weed control on a custom NC-specific schedule.

How to Boost Curb Appeal in Raleigh, NC

Raleigh's competitive real estate market makes curb appeal landscaping a smart investment. Start with fresh mulch in flower beds — it instantly upgrades any Raleigh home. Add defined edging along driveways and walkways. Install low-maintenance native NC plants like Knockout Roses and Crepe Myrtles. Our Raleigh landscape design team specializes in curb appeal transformations that increase property value across Wake County.
